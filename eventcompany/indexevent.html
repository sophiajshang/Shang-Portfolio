About Us

As an up-and-coming event planner in the DC region Cross works with a wide range of clientele from the private sector to the government. They provide expert planning services for all occasion and events. In a short seven years Cross Event Planning has propelled itself into the top tier of event planners and coordinators in the DC region. With a full staff of 75 employees Cross can meet the needs of any size event with speed and efficiency. Cross primarily handles events for political parties, political lobbies, NGO’s, government and private institutions, universities, non-profits, and private companies. Clients include Democratic National Party, The Kato Institute, The Brookings Institute, American University, Georgetown University, Virginia Commonwealth University, Lockheed Martin, The Smithsonian, Hirschhorn Museum, National Institute of Health, Center for Disease Control, The Nationals and many more.
